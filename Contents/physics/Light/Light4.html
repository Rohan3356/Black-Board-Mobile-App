L4: Image formation by concave mirror

The type of image formed by a concave mirror depends on the position of the object kept in front of the mirror. We can place the object at following places

  1. Between pole P and focus F
  2. At the focus
  3. Between focus F and centre of curvature C
  4. At the centre of curvature
  5. Beyond center of curvature
  6. At far off distances called infinity and cannot be shown in the figures.
