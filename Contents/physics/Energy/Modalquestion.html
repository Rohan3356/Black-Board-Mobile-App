Physics

Model Question

Two marks questions.

  1. The use of dry wood as domestic fuel is not considered as good. State two reasons for it.
  2. What are the three disadvantages of burning fossil fuels?
  3. Name four sources of energy which are pollution free.
  4. Why is the solar cooker box covered with a plane glass plate?
  5. Name the main constituent of biogas and Name two combustible components of biogas.
  6. Why does acid rain happen?
  7. Name four conventional sources of energy.
  8. State the reason for calling fossil fuels as non-renewable source of energy.
  9. Name the major constituent in natural gas.

Four marks questions.

  1. Suggest two materials that can be used to produce bio – gas. Mention two uses of bio – gas and two advantages of bio – gas plant.
  2. How charcoal is better fuel than wood, why? How does bio-gas plant help to reduce the problem of pollution?
  3. Name two semiconductors used in solar cell. What is solar cell pane? State two main advantages of solar panel.
  4. Why is not possible to make use of solar cells to meet all our energy needs? State at least three reasons to support your answer.
  5. What is bio-gas? Why is bio-gas considered an ideal fuel for domestic use?
  6. Short Answer any two:
    1. What is a wind energy farm?
    2. Mention the steps involved in generating electricity from such farms.
    3. State two steps in the process of generation of electricity from a windmill.
  7. What is the importance of hydro power plants in India? Describe how electric energy is generated in such plants.
